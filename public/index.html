Us

This is Kayley. And this is Abdul. Their story started five years ago in Barcelona. They couldn’t haven been more different, but then again isn’t that how most great love stories start?

Our Story

Abdul and Kayley first locked eyes across a plate of free parmesan garlic Buffalo Wild Wings. They had both showed up for the “Visualizing Information” study abroad program info session. Abdul didn’t talk to Kayley during the meeting, but decided not to take the few more wings he had been eying in case she was watching. He decided that day he was going to Spain.

Apr 2012

In Barcelona, Kayley and Abdul toured amazing sites for their class. They toured Sagrada Familia, traveled to a monastery via cable car, and bonded over a mutual love of a Dali painting. Sparks didn’t fly immediately, but two weeks into the five-week trip, Kayley started falling for Abdul’s mischievous smile and charm. Madrid provided the perfect backdrop for the unexpected romance.

June 2012

Abdul flew back home to North Carolina, while Kayley flew back to Michigan to finish her last year and a half at MSU. They Facetimed and texted while Abdul looked for jobs in NC. It felt like fate when he got a job offer in Michigan, so Abdul packed up and moved back.

Aug 2012

Over the next three years, Kayley and Abdul hung out on the PIKE fire truck at tailgates, adopted a stray cat from the cottage in Crystal, did long distance while Abdul lived in St. Louis, MO for his startup company, and...

Oct 2012

On Oct. 9, 2015, Abdul and Kayley drove up to Traverse City to spend a weekend wine tasting, just the two of them. They worked their way up Old Mission Peninsula, ending at Kayley’s favorite winery, Bower’s Harbor. At almost exactly sunset, as the two were sitting at a bistro table nestled in the vineyard, a woman approached them and asked if they wanted their picture taken. Abdul seized the photo opportunity and got down on one knee just as the woman started snapping away. She ended up being a professional photographer who was on a wine-weekend with her girlfriends!

Oct 2015

Almost five years exactly from when they first met, Abdul and Kayley will tie the knot at Michigan State University.

June 24, 2017
